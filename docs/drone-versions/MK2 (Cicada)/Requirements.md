### MK2 (Cicada) Mission Requirements

Primary Mission:

* Demonstrate stable and repeatable flight.

Success Criteria:

* Take off safely.
* Hover under pilot control.
* Perform basic maneuvers.
* Land safely.
* Achieve a minimum flight time of 10 minutes.
* Complete multiple flights without structural or electrical failures.

Non-Goals:

* Autonomous navigation.
* Computer vision.
* External payloads.
* Obstacle avoidance.
* Mapping.
* SLAM.
* AI processing.
