## MK2 Mission

### Primary objective:
- Carry itself and fly safely for at least 10 minutes.

### Optional objectives:
- 15-minute flight time.
- Stable manual flight.
- Repeatable takeoff and landing.
- Validation of the future MK3 airframe.

### Success Criteria:
- Take off safely.
- Hover under pilot control.
- Perform basic maneuvers.
- Land safely.
- Achieve a minimum flight time of 10 minutes.
- Complete multiple flights without structural or electrical failures.
