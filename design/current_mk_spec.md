# Current MK Specification

## Active MK
MK0 (Bill)

## Purpose
MK0 is a design and research prototype used for concept exploration, digital modeling, and documentation.

## Scope
- Digital design concepts
- Design exploration
- Research and documentation

## Out of Scope
- Flight capability
- Electronics integration
- Physical testing

## Rule of Thumb
Only one MK may be active at a time. Future MK versions are conceptual until promoted to active development.
